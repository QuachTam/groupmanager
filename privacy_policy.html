When you use SmartGroup, we may collect the following information:

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

4. Social Media Login

When you choose to sign in using Google or Facebook:

  • We only request access to your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture)

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored securely and we use industry-standard encryption methods to protect data transmission.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
